
- PCs travel to **Kymball** and seek help from the **Esoteric Order of the Palatine Eye**.
- While waiting, they investigate a series of **vampire murders**.
- They find the Whispering Way hideout abandoned and meet **Quinley Basdel** (a dhampir).
- Quinley introduces them to **Luvick Siervage**, who offers information on the Whispering Way if they solve the murders.
- Investigation in the **Vampire Underground** reveals **Radvir Giovanni** is the killer.
- Radvir leads the PCs to **Aisa and Hetna** at the **Abbey of Sante-Lymirin**.
- After defeating the witches, the PCs learn their potion is **unrelated to the Whispering Way's ritual**.
- The Whispering Way has moved to **Virlych** and kidnapped **Count Lucinean Galdana of Amaans**.
- Rescuing the count sets up the final adventure, **Shadows of Gallowspire**.

### Moribund Key

The **Moribund Key** is a silver medallion carried by high-ranking members of the **Whispering Way**, serving as both an identification token and a magical key to the cult's hidden sanctuaries.
#### Appearance

- Silver medallion engraved with a skull.
- The skull's eyes are two gray, burned-out **ioun stones**.
- Small openings ring the outer edge.
- Rotating the skull aligns hidden runes inside the medallion.

#### Function

The key's rune combinations correspond to magical symbols engraved on important Whispering Way objects, such as:

- Doors
- Statues
- Paintings

Using the correct rune combination can activate or bypass magical effects, including:

- Wards
- Programmed images
- Arcane locks

#### Magical Properties

The bearer can cast:

| Spell              |    Uses |
| ------------------ | ------: |
| **Message**        | At will |
| **Spectral Hand**  |   3/day |
| **Vampiric Touch** |   3/day |

Additionally:

- The skull's eyes glow whenever **undead** are within **60 feet**.

#### Curse

Any **good-aligned** creature carrying the Moribund Key gains **looses 1 HP and 1 Hope slot** as long as the medallion is carried on you

It cannot be removed or suppressed while the creature remains in possession of the key.
